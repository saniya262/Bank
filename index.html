Online Bank

In the most basic terms, online banking is the act of conducting financial transactions via the internet. Instead of visiting a local bank branch to perform banking tasks such as making a deposit, transferring money or paying your bills, you handle everything with your financial institution through a secure website or app. With online banking, you can manage your accounts online at any time—day or night—even outside of banking hours and on bank holidays. Depending on the nature of your transaction and the hours your bank operates, however, you may need to wait for certain transactions, such as check deposits, to be finalized.financial institution through a secure website or app.
